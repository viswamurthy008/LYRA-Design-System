# Content & Microcopy

Voice: **plain, direct, and calm.** Write like a helpful colleague, not a mascot or a lawyer.
Address the user as "you"; refer to the product's actions in first person only in confirmations
("We've emailed you a receipt").

## Capitalization

- **Sentence case everywhere** — buttons, titles, labels, menu items ("Save changes", not
  "Save Changes").
- Product and feature names keep their brand casing (Storybook, GitHub).
- Never ALL CAPS in copy; the `Overline` text style handles visual uppercase via CSS.

## Buttons & actions

- Verb first, outcome clear: **"Save changes"**, **"Delete project"**, **"Invite team"**.
  Avoid vague labels ("OK", "Yes", "Submit").
- Destructive buttons name the object: "Delete report", and the confirming Modal repeats it.
- One primary button per view. Secondary actions use the secondary/ghost variants.
- Pairs are verb + escape: "Save changes / Cancel" — never "Yes / No".

## Errors

- Say **what happened + how to fix it**, in that order: "Enter a valid email address."
  not "Invalid input!"
- No blame ("you failed to…"), no jargon (error codes go in a collapsible detail, not the headline).
- Field errors live in `FormField error` next to the field. Page-level failures use `Alert`.
- Never joke in error messages.

## Empty states

- Lead with what belongs here, then the action: "No projects yet — create your first one."
- Always pair `EmptyState` with a button or link; a dead end is a bug.

## Labels, hints & placeholders

- Labels are nouns ("Email", "Team"), 1–3 words, no colons.
- Hints add what the label can't: format, consequences ("Used for sign-in and receipts.").
- Placeholders show **format examples only** ("you@example.com") — never instructions,
  because they vanish on input.

## Numbers, dates & truncation

- Abbreviate large numbers in tiles ("$48.2k"), full numbers in tables.
- Relative time under 7 days ("2h ago"), absolute after ("Jul 1, 2026").
- Truncate with an ellipsis and keep the full value in a Tooltip.

## Terminology

Pick one term per concept and stick to it: **Delete** (permanent) vs **Remove** (from a list) vs
**Archive** (recoverable); **Sign in / Sign out** (not "login"); **Team** (not "workspace/org"
interchangeably).
